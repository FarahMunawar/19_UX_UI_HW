About Me

Hi, My name is Farah Munawar. I am fine artist turned UX/UI Designer. My design ethos consist of prioritising user needs and goals.Aims to result in intuitive and user centric experience.It's equally crucial for these efforts to be genuine and aligned with the company's core values.

Education

  • Collaboration & Communication:
    Effectively collaborating between teams, stakeholders, and clients, to communicate design decisions and recommendations is been my strong suit as a designer.
  • User Research:
    My qualitative and quantitative research helps gain insights into user behaviors, needs, and preferences.
  • Empathy:
    My design objective is to create communication through senses and emotions, empathising with users and being in their shoes and thinking with them.
  • Analytical Thinking:
    Analysing data, metrics, and user feedback to inform design decisions and improvements considering user needs and goals are core of my design process.
  • Information Architecture:
    Designing or Redesigning, structuring information in a coherent manner for easy user understanding and navigation prioritising user pain points.
  • Problem Solving:
    Problem solving helps understand, define then create, in my design process by exploring multiple prespective, brainstrom ideas and consider various design alternatives to find the most effective solution which then analyse and evaluate critically.
  • Visual Design:
    Aesthetic sensibility and proficiency in creating visually appealing and cohesive designs that align with user expectations.
  • Interaction Design:
    Interaction design skills works as a bridge between users and digital interface.
  • Prototyping:
